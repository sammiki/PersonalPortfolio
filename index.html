About

My name is Samantha King, and I am a student of Computer Science at Portland State University. I am captivated by the boundless possibilities of the computing universe and the power to shape the intangible into functional and innovative solutions.

Throughout my journey, I have honed my expertise in several programming languages, including Python, C#, and C++. Continually expanding my skill set, I consistently demonstrate an agile capacity to adapt to unfamiliar programming languages.

My passion for art and design has been instrumental in my pursuit of game development. By combining my programming skills with a keen eye for aesthetics, I am able to craft immersive and visually stunning gaming experiences. In addition to my programming abilities, I have a strong grasp of linear algebra, a field that plays a vital role in computer graphics and simulation.

As a lifelong learner, I am constantly exploring new technologies and programming paradigms to push the boundaries of what is possible. My approach to problem-solving is rooted in analytical thinking and attention to detail. With an unwavering passion for the intersection of computing and design, I am continuously inspired to explore new frontiers and harness the limitless potential that lies within this ever-evolving realm.
